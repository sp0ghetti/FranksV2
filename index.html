My Name Is Franks

Canadian recording artist.

class="fr" height="250" width="250"/>

Aaron Frank prides himself on unique writing, and catchy hooks. His music is heavily inspired by his upbringing in Surrey, BC- he grew up in the BC housing system with his brother, and mother. Fighting the odds made Franks who he is today.
